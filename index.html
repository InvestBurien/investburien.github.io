Burien Local Investment Opportunities Network

We're a small and growing group of individuals interested in investing locally and sustainably in Burien, WA. While we have bigger plans for the future our goal now is to build a community to support local, small business in Burien.

All investments are done by individuals, we don't advise or broker any agreements. We simply put everyone in touch through events and introductions.

Sign-Up to Our E-mail List

We'll send you updates and opportunities, nothing else. Please note that membership is limited to Burien and Normandy Park Residents at this time. We only work with businesses and start-ups in the City of Burien.
